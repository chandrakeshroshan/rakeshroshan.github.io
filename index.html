About Dr. S. Chand Rakesh Roshan

Dr. S. Chand Rakesh Roshan is a distinguished academician, researcher, and educator specializing in computational materials science, condensed matter physics and ICT Tools in Education. With a strong foundation in theoretical physics, he has contributed significantly to the fields of energy materials, thermoelectric materials, and electronic structure calculations. His research work has been published in renowned international journals, and he has presented at prestigious conferences worldwide.

Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at RGUKT Basar, Dr. Roshan has a proven track record in academic leadership, curriculum development, and mentoring students . He has organized numerous faculty development programs, workshops, and conferences to foster knowledge dissemination and professional growth in the scientific community.

Dr. Roshan is an active member of various esteemed professional organizations and serves as a reviewer for reputed scientific journals. His dedication to academic excellence, innovation, and interdisciplinary research makes him a leading figure in the advancement of materials science and engineering .
